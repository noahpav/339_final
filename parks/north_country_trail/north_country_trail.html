North Country Trail

The North Country National Scenic Trail (NCT) is a hiking trail that spans an impressive 4,800 miles across eight states, from Vermont to North Dakota. In Michigan, the trail stretches over 1,100 miles, offering hikers the chance to experience the state’s diverse landscapes, including serene forests, rugged shorelines, and rolling hills. Whether you're seeking a short day hike or a multi-day backpacking adventure, the NCT provides an unparalleled opportunity to connect with Michigan’s natural beauty.

What They Offer

  • Hiking: Traverse the trail’s many sections, ranging from short loops to challenging multi-day treks. Popular segments include the Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore: Stunning views of Lake Superior and colorful cliffs, the Manistee National Forest: Quiet woodlands and riverside trails, and the Porcupine Mountains: Rugged terrain and breathtaking vistas.
  • Camping: Many segments of the trail feature nearby campgrounds or backcountry camping opportunities, allowing for immersive multi-day trips.
  • Wildlife Watching: Spot a variety of wildlife, including deer, black bears, and countless bird species.
  • Photography: Capture Michigan’s natural beauty, from dense forests to sparkling lakeshores.
  • Snowshoeing and Cross-Country Skiing: In the winter, enjoy sections of the trail transformed into snowy wonderlands.

Some History

Designated as a National Scenic Trail in 1980, the North Country Trail was established to promote outdoor recreation and preserve America’s natural and cultural heritage. Michigan plays a crucial role in the trail’s journey, hosting the longest segment within any single state. Over the decades, the trail has grown through the efforts of dedicated volunteers and partnerships with local communities and public lands.

The NCT connects numerous iconic destinations in Michigan, including Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore, the Huron-Manistee National Forests, and Tahquamenon Falls. It is part of a larger network of national trails designed to celebrate the country’s natural beauty and provide outdoor enthusiasts with unparalleled experiences.
